Frequently Asked Questions

How do I order prescription medicines?

To order prescription medicines, add the item to your cart and you will be prompted to upload a valid doctor's prescription during checkout.

How fast is the delivery?

We offer express 2-hour delivery in major cities and standard 1-2 day delivery in other areas. You can check exact timings using the pincode checker on the product page.

What is the Monthly Subscription?

The monthly subscription feature automatically reorders your selected medicines every month, ensuring you never run out of regular medication. You also get a 10% discount on subscribed items.
