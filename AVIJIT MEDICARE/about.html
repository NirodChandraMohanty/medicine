Our Journey

Two decades of commitment towards revolutionizing healthcare access in India.

2003

The Foundation

Opened our first brick-and-mortar pharmacy in Baleswar with a vision to provide authentic medicines and 24/7 care.

2010

City Expansion

Expanded our footprint to 5 key locations across the district, introducing rapid services for medical emergencies.

2020

Digital Pivot

2026
